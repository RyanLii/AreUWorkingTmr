# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions.
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact the maintainer directly.

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this code.
